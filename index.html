Tincture reads what the live ladder is actually playing and lets you grab any pick in one click. For the top picks that's now a real, loadable .build — reconstructed from a representative public-ladder character (passives, gems and items, with the source character credited in the file) and dropped straight into PoE 2's in-game Build Planner. For picks we haven't reconstructed yet, Decant exports an honest labelled template rather than a fabricated build. Either way you go from "what should I play?" to a starting point in one click.

  1. 1

    Decant a pick

    Press Decant on the headline build or any ledger row. For a reconstructed pick you get a real, loadable .build — passives, gems and items from a credited public-ladder character. For one we haven't reconstructed yet, you get a labelled .txt template: the ascendancy, its ladder share, the sample, the playstyle, and a guide pointer.

  2. 2

    Load it in-game

    Connect your BuildPlanner folder once and the .build saves straight into Documents\My Games\Path of Exile 2\BuildPlanner — otherwise it downloads and you drop it in. Open the in-game Build Planner to follow it. A .txt template isn't loadable, so don't rename it to .build (the game would silently refuse it) — pair it with a community guide instead.

  3. 3

    Always honest no fabrication

    Every reconstructed .build credits the exact source character, account and poe.ninja link — never bylined as ours. Picks without a real reconstruction fall back to the clearly-labelled template; Tincture never writes a fake .build the game would silently refuse.

No account, no login, nothing tracked — Tincture only ever writes to the one folder you choose, right in your browser. GGG does not curate or rank builds; judge any pick on its merits.

The Prescription

Answer a few questions and Tincture matches you to ascendancies that fit — ranked, each with an honest reason. Your answers live only in the link, so the result is shareable.

Matching is editorial guidance — from the playstyle tags plus the meta's weapons and median EHP/DPS — a starting shortlist, not a power ranking. Every result links to the honest ledger row and Decant.
